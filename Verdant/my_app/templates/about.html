About Verdant

Verdant is a fun and interactive Django web application that allows users to manage their own virtual garden. With Verdant, you can grow, care for, and track your plants, making plant care both engaging and educational.

Features

  • User Management: Sign up, login, and manage your personal garden.
  • Plant Care: Add new plants, track their growth, and perform care actions like watering, fertilizing, and pruning.
  • Growth System: Watch your plants grow through stages—from seedling to flowering—based on your care and attention.
  • Health Tracking: Each plant has a health status that reflects its current condition, encouraging regular care.
  • Accessories: Attach decorative or functional accessories to your plants to enhance their growth or appearance.
  • Gamification: Earn badges and achievements for consistently caring for your plants and reaching full growth stages.

Why Verdant?

Verdant encourages daily interaction and responsibility, making it perfect for plant enthusiasts and anyone who wants to enjoy the satisfaction of nurturing virtual greenery. Whether you’re learning about plant care or just enjoying a relaxing virtual garden, Verdant makes it simple and fun.

Tech Stack

Verdant is built with Django 5.x and Python 3.11 for the backend, using SQLite (development) or PostgreSQL (production) as the database. The frontend is built with HTML, CSS, and Django templates, with static assets organized in the /static/ folder.

Verdant uses background tasks to automatically update plant health and growth stages, providing a dynamic and interactive experience.
